Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Unbinge is built with one principle: your data stays on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, what we do and don't collect.

1. What the extension stores

All Unbinge settings — your age profile, Education Mode toggle, distraction filters, locally approved channels, and theme — are saved in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device.

2. What we do not collect

  • No names, emails, or account details — there is no sign-up.
  • No browsing history or watch history.
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • No third-party trackers or analytics on the extension.

3. Channel requests

When you submit a channel for global review, only the channel URL is sent to our server. No personal information is attached to the request.

4. Whitelist sync

When the extension syncs the approved channel list, your browser sends an API key and a hashed version of your IP address (SHA-256) for rate-limiting only. We never store raw IP addresses and cannot identify you from the hash.

5. Uninstall feedback

If you choose to leave feedback when uninstalling, the reason and optional message you provide are stored on our server to help improve Unbinge. Submission is entirely optional and anonymous.

6. This website

The unbinge.watch website does not use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers. Standard server logs (IP, timestamp, requested path) may be kept by Hostinger for security and abuse prevention, as required by hosting policy.

7. Children's privacy

Unbinge is designed for families and children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else. If you believe a child has shared data with us, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Data retention

Channel requests and uninstall feedback are retained for as long as useful for improving the service. You may request deletion of any feedback you submitted by emailing us.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@unbinge.watch or visit our contact page.
